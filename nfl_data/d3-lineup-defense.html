Cover 0: 6 rushers (4 DL + 2 LB). Every eligible receiver is manned up. Left over: NOBODY. No safety help. High risk/high reward. If any receiver beats his man, it's a touchdown. But 6 rushers vs 5 blockers means free rusher at QB.

Summary

Your formation has 5 eligible receivers: 3 WRs + TE + RB

Defense must account for all 5. With 4 rushing, that leaves 7 in coverage:
• 5 defenders man up on receivers
• 2 "free" defenders for robber/spy/deep help

To get more rushers, defense sacrifices help:
• 5 rush = 1 free defender
• 6 rush (Cover 0) = 0 free, pure man, big play risk

The unbalanced look forces the defense to shift and communicate assignments. Any confusion = easy completion.
