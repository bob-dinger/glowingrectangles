Cover 0: 6 rushers vs 6 blockers. Everyone manned up. Huge risk: The isolated center creates a free lane. Both LBs blitzing through the A-gaps have a clear path. But if offense gets it out quick or anyone beats their man = touchdown.

Summary

This formation creates problems:

• Isolated center - Easy to exploit gaps on either side
• Split blockers - Defense can't align traditionally
• Numbers advantage right - 4 OL + TE + RB can dominate vs run
• Trips left with OL - Can run screens/sweeps with lead blocker

Defense has to pick their poison:
• Shift to trips = give up the run right
• Stack the box right = trips side has no help
• Blitz = one missed tackle is 6 points
