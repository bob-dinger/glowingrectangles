The Problem of Induction
Same brain, dangerous conclusions

The same mental shortcut that helps us learn from one switch also leads us astray.

A turkey is fed every day for 364 days. Each feeding increases its confidence that the farmer is a friend.

On day 365, the turkey's confidence is at an all-time high. It's also Thanksgiving.

"The fact that the sun has risen every day of your life doesn't prove it will rise tomorrow."
