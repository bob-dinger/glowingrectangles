Survivorship Bias

The Baltimore Stockbroker is a classic thought experiment illustrating survivorship bias‚Äîthe logical error of focusing on winners while ignoring the losers who are no longer visible.

The broker has no skill. By sending opposite predictions to different groups, half will always be "right." After 7 rounds, someone is guaranteed to have received 7 correct predictions.
