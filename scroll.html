Fuck The Scroll

The infinite scroll taught you that there's always more. Another post. Another take. Another thing you haven't seen yet. It trained your brain to never stop, never be satisfied, never sit with anything.

You don't finish things anymore. You consume the first 30 seconds, then move on. Articles, videos, conversations, relationships. There might be something better just below.

Understanding requires stillness. It requires reading something twice. Thinking about it. Letting it sit. The scroll made that feel like a waste of time. Why think when you could consume?

This page doesn't scroll. This is everything. There is nothing below. No more content. No comments section. No related posts. No infinite feed of alternatives.

The scroll isn't free. You pay with your attention, your depth, and your ability to be satisfied.
This is it. Sit with it.
